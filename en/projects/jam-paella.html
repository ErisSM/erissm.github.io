Paella Loca

Key words: GameJam, Unity, VR

Description

This game was developed for a 5-day Game Jam organized by U-Tad that had "Spanish gastronomy" as its theme.
We developed a frenetic party game, set in a bullring, that involved making paellas with ingredients thrown by the audience. The player would have to catch the correct ingredients mid-air(rice, chicken, beans, and lemons), while ignoring other objects and food thrown, in order to cook as many paellas as possible within the time limit. Once the time was up, a bull would appear and it would start chasing the player. The player could still play and try to complete paellas, but once the bull catches them, the game would end.

Role in the project

We developed the game as a team of five people. We brainstormed together to define and design the game, and then we made a list of tasks to be completed and divided them up among us. As part of the team, I developed various game systems, which I later shared with the rest of the team so everyone could know how everything worked.
Since all 5 members of our team were programmers, most of the 3D models & art used in the game came from free asset packs.
